About Young Motors Geraldton

OUTSTANDING SERVICE SINCE 1928

Here at Young Motors, we continue to exceed the expectations of our customers.

A proud local family owned and operated business, we pride ourselves on our professional and efficient service to the people of the Midwest and beyond. With such history comes much responsibility, hence our focus on staff training, whether it be in service, vehicle detailing, sales or customer care. All of our staff are required to attend ongoing manufacturer backed training for customer service, Mitsubishi, Mazda and Holden.

SERVICES ON OFFER

Holden, Mazda and Mitsubishi – you’ll find them all at Young Motors, Geraldton.

At any time, choose from hundreds of new and used vehicles or take advantage of our state-of-the art service and parts facilities.

Take advantage of our in-house finance products through our association with both St George Bank and Macquarie Leasing. At Young Motors, we pride ourselves on offering the very highest level of service in a professional and efficient manner. Same day approvals and vehicle deliveries happen every day at Young’s.

The Young Motors experience only starts at the sale, once part of the family our team are committed to help and support in any way possible.

For more information or to book a test drive, please contact a friendly member of our team today.
